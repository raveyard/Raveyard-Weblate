Raveyard


This is how we came to be.


In October 2015 we started studying media studies in Bayreuth, Germany. After a couple of months of getting to know everybody in the class we had a solid foundation of what should later be called "RaveYard" - friendship.
This close group of friends then attended several courses together and formed a group in multiple temporary projects like GameJams and film making competitions. We soon noticed our interdisciplinary skillsets and backgrounds, as well as experiences we were able to make before studying in Bayreuth, corresponded in perfect harmony when working together on said projects. This lead us to give our group a name which would later be our professional identity - Team Raveyard. Now we work on several computer game or film projects, either for university or professionally, to inspire others or simply for immersive enjoyment.

We'll be going our way where nobody else may have been, and leave a trail for everybody else to hopefully follow along.
